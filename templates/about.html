About Crop Yield Prediction App

This application helps in predicting the yield of crops based on various environmental and biological factors. The inputs include average raining days, clone size, temperature ranges, and types of bees affecting pollination. Our goal is to assist farmers in making informed decisions to maximize their crop yield.

Features:

  • Easy to use input form
  • Accurate yield predictions
  • Useful insights for farmers
